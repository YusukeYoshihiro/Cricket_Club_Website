Cricket is one of the most enjoyable sports for women.
If you are interested please contact us below!!

TEAM

We are a lively, friendly group of women who have one shared love - CRICKET! We have some players with a season's experience who are becoming a dab hand with the bat and ball, but we also welcome complete beginners or anyone who used to play at school or has previous experience and wants to play the best summer sport there is! If you are a woman or a girl aged 12 and over and would like to play or know any friends who would like to join please contact us and we will be happy to welcome you!

SCHEDULE

Please check this page in April 2020 for the league schedule. The BC Mainland Cricket League is reponsible for the 2020 schedule. Most games will played on Sundays.

For more information please contact the club at the following email: womenscricket@westvancouvercricketclub.ca

TRAINNIG

With the regular season starting on the last weekend of April, outdoor training commences once the weather permits.

This is usually the first week of April. Please check this page in the spring for the training program. We have 2 permanent nets and one portable net for centre wicket practice.
