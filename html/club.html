ABOUT WVCC

The West Vancouver Cricket Club, based at Hugo Ray Park in West Vancouver is one of the largest cricket clubs in Canada. It is rated the best cricketing facility in Western Canada.


It is our aim to develop a strong and successful club both on and off the field and to provide an enjoyable environment for all members, guests and spectators.


Our club field will field seven (7) competitive league teams playing in the British Columbia Mainland Cricket League (BCMCL) for the 2016 Season. The top teams play One Day International (ODI) while the rest of the teams play limited 45 overs. Games are played on Saturdays and Sundays. The regular season starts on the last weekend of April and runs to the Labour Day weekend. The club also participates in a Pre-Season Twenty20 Cricket Tournament which runs for 5 weekends prior to the start of the regular season.

Other cricket events/tournaments include:

  • Social Over 40's Cricket (mid week)
  • Junior Cricket - Under 12's and Under 17's programs
  • High peformance cricket programs
  • Summer Cricket Camp (July 4-8, 2016) at Hugo Ray Park
  • Women's Cricket (May to August)
  • The club supports and provides coaches for School Cricket
  • Social games during the season
  • Mid-week Twenty20 Cricket
  • 6-A-Side Tournaments
  • Matches against touring teams
  • Community and social cricket on Fridays from 6-8 pm (June - August)

Facilities and equipment

Our club's home, Hugo Ray Park, has great facilities including 2 outdoor nets and 2 grounds - changing rooms with showers and ample free parking. The club is fortunate to have a clubhouse for use by members and guests. The clubhouse is located adjacent to the two cricket grounds and has numerous amenities including a licensed bar, a pool table and a ping pong table, kitchen, BBQ, and lounge and bar areas leading onto balconies which overlook the grounds. The clubhouse is also available for rental.

We are always looking for new members and if you and/or any of your friends are interested in playing cricket feel free to contact us!

PROGRAMS

JUNIORS

West Vancouver Cricket Club has the largest junior cricket program in Vancouver with three teams playing. Over 40 young players from across Metro Vancouver are enrolled in our program and play in the spring / summer season.

LEAGUE

With the regular season starting on the last weekend of April, outdoor training commences once the weather permits. This is usually the first week of April. Please check this page in the spring for the training program.

WOMEN'S

Cricket is one of the most enjoyable sports for women. If you are a woman or a girl aged 12 and over and would like to play or know any friends who would like to join please contact us and we will be happy to welcome you!

FACILITIES

CLUBHOUSE
Located in West Vancouver with views of the scenic North Shore mountains, Hugo Ray Park is home to the West Vancouver Cricket Club and their clubhouse. The fields are used primarily for cricket in the summer with soccer being played in the winter months.
LICENSED BAR
The upstairs bar and lounge is ideal for special occasions such as wedding receptions,birthday parties, anniversaries, cocktail receptions and various other private and promotional events.
MEETING ROOM
Groups can host seminars and it is the perfect alternative for corporate meetings. Our downstairs board room can accommodate up to 25 people for a meeting or training session.
