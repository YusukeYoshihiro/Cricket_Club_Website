TRAINING

 
 

Outdoor Training

Starts in early April at Hugo Ray Park (weather dependent).

With the regular season starting on the last weekend of April, outdoor training commences once the weather permits.

This is usually the first week of April. Please check this page in the spring for the training program.

We have 2 permanent nets and one portable net for centre wicket practice.


Club practices are held as follows:

  • Monday - wowen's training (6:00 pm- 8:00 pm)
  • Tuesday - club practice (5:30 pm - 8:00 pm)
  • Thursday - optional club practice (needs to be coordinated with the junior cricket coordinator)
  • Thursdays - junior program (6:00 pm - 8:00 pm)

INDOOR TRAINING

Winter 2018

January 20 to mid-March

6:00 pm to 8:00 pm

50 Full indoor training program (8 sessions)

Surrey

Indoor training will begin on Saturday, January 20th, 2018. Indoor training will continue until the weather improves and we can move outdoors. The indoor training sessions are open to all members, new and potential players for the 2018 season.

For insurance and legal purposes, all participants MUST be insured by registering with the Club and BCMCL. If you played last season, you are still covered until February 1, 2018. Registration forms will be available for participants to sign at the indoor centre. New players must sign registration form to participate.

The pre-season Twenty20 cricket will start on March 17 and the regular season begins in late April. The regular season is 19 weeks in duration with 18 matches - all limited overs games. That means that each team will have one bye during the regular season. The playoffs start on the first weekend in September.
